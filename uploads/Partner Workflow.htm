Partner Workflow & Requirements Blueprint
Online Olympiad / Talent Exam Portal

1. Executive Summary

This document details the functional blueprint for the Partner stakeholder journey within the Online Olympiad / Talent Exam Portal. It provides a comprehensive outline of the end-to-end partner experience, focusing on B2B channel partners and resellers who manage assigned institutions, drive student registrations, and monitor campaign performance. The primary objective of this module is to empower partners with self-serve dashboard tools, transparent attribution, and real-time visibility into conversion metrics and financial payouts, while ensuring operational compliance.

2. Scope and Assumptions

  • In Scope: Partner onboarding, referral link generation, assigned institution mapping, registration/conversion tracking, analytics visibility (scorecards, rank/percentiles), and payout/commission lifecycles.
  • Out of Scope: Student examination interface, direct school administrative configurations, and any portal workflows related to student guardians.
  • Assumptions:
    • The core payment model is direct online payment by the student via the portal.
    • Partners operate entirely in English.
    • Communication with partners relies exclusively on automated emails and in-app dashboard alerts.
    • Partners have a dedicated helpdesk for support escalations.

3. Partner Persona and Business Objectives

Persona: Channel Partners, Resellers, and Corporate/Institutional Liaisons.

Core Objectives:

  • Onboard seamlessly and receive institutional assignments from the platform admin.
  • Generate and distribute unique referral/campaign links to their assigned schools.
  • Maximize student sign-ups and track paid conversions in real-time.
  • Monitor student performance (analytics, certificates) to demonstrate value to assigned institutions.
  • Ensure accurate calculation and timely settlement of commissions.

4. Partner Lifecycle at a Glance

  1. Acquisition & Onboarding: Partner signs up, is vetted, and approved by Portal Admin.
  2. Mapping: Portal Admin assigns specific institutions to the Partner's portfolio.
  3. Campaign Activation: Partner generates trackable referral links for assigned institutions.
  4. Conversion Tracking: Partner monitors student sign-ups and direct payments in real-time.
  5. Exam & Result Cycle: Partner views participation rates and aggregate/student performance post-exam.
  6. Settlement: Partner tracks commission statements, raises disputes if necessary, and marks payouts as received.

Recommended Partner Status Labels: Draft, Submitted, Pending Review, Active, Restricted, Suspended, Inactive, Closed.

Recommended Commission Statement States: Draft, Under Review, Approved, Scheduled, Paid, Disputed, Adjusted.

5. Detailed End-to-End Partner Workflow by Stage

5.1 Lead / Institution Assignment

  • Goal: Link the partner to their operational territory or specific schools.
  • Trigger: Partner agreement is signed offline or via standard application.
  • Main Actions: Admin searches and assigns existing school profiles to the partner ID.
  • System Behavior: Creates a mapped relationship in the database. Updates Partner's "Assigned Institutions" widget.
  • Outputs: Notification to Partner: "New institutions added to your portfolio."
  • Failure Points: Duplicate institution mapping (two partners claiming the same school); orphaned schools.

5.2 Partner Onboarding and Approval

  • Goal: Secure and authenticate partner access to the portal.
  • Trigger: Partner clicks "Register as Partner" on the main portal.
  • Main Actions: Submit organization details, tax ID, and banking info. Await admin approval.
  • System Behavior: Account in "Pending Approval" state. Admin review triggers "Active" state.
  • Outputs: Welcome email with login credentials.
  • Failure Points: Rejection due to missing tax documents; emails bouncing.

5.3 Account Activation and Dashboard Setup

  • Goal: Partner configures internal user roles (Admin, Finance, Account Manager).
  • Trigger: First successful login.
  • Main Actions: Complete profile, accept terms, invite sub-users.
  • System Behavior: Provisions role-based access to the dashboard.
  • Outputs: Active dashboard interface.
  • Failure Points: Inactive partner accounts due to lost passwords; role-based access conflicts.

5.4 Referral / Campaign Setup

  • Goal: Generate unique identifiers to attribute student sign-ups to the partner.
  • Trigger: Partner selects an assigned institution and clicks "Create Campaign Link."
  • Main Actions: Input campaign name, select exam category, set expiry date (optional).
  • System Behavior: System generates a unique URL embedding the Partner ID and Institution ID.
  • Outputs: Shareable URL; downloadable QR code.
  • Failure Points: Expired referral links being circulated; broken URLs due to formatting errors.

5.5 Institution Mapping and Management

  • Goal: Allow partners to view the health of their assigned schools.
  • Trigger: Routine dashboard check.
  • Main Actions: Filter assigned schools, view contact details, view "total students registered."
  • System Behavior: Aggregates student registration data rolling up to the mapped Institution ID.
  • Outputs: Institution-level summary report.
  • Failure Points: Institution changes name or merges, causing mapping discrepancies.

5.6 Registration and Conversion Tracking

  • Goal: Track the funnel from click -> signup -> paid registration.
  • Trigger: Students use the referral link to register and pay directly online.
  • Main Actions: Partner views "Conversion Funnel" tab.
  • System Behavior: Increments lead count and paid conversion count. Calculates pending commission based on contract rate.
  • Outputs: Real-time conversion metrics widget.
  • Failure Points: Disputed attribution (student used a different link); student refund requests canceling a previously recorded conversion.

5.7 Exam Cycle Monitoring

  • Goal: Monitor exam participation for referred students.
  • Trigger: Exam day commences.
  • Main Actions: View live participation rates (Registered vs. Appeared).
  • System Behavior: Pulls attendance flags from the core exam engine.
  • Outputs: Attendance percentage per assigned institution.
  • Failure Points: Technical dropouts skewing attendance metrics.

5.8 Result and Performance Visibility

  • Goal: Provide partners with proof of value (scorecards, ranks).
  • Trigger: Portal admin publishes normalized results.
  • Main Actions: Partner downloads detailed analytics, percentiles, and aggregate certificates for their schools.
  • System Behavior: Generates restricted read-only views of student scorecards and school-level benchmarking.
  • Outputs: Exportable performance reports (PDF/CSV).
  • Failure Points: Privacy constraints (student opting out of data sharing) causing reporting gaps.

5.9 Payout and Commission Workflow

  • Goal: Transparent financial settlement.
  • Trigger: End of month / End of exam cycle.
  • Main Actions: Partner views auto-generated commission statement.
  • System Behavior: System tallies (Total Conversions - Refunds) * Commission Rate, freezes the statement period, and moves the statement through Draft -> Under Review -> Approved -> Scheduled -> Paid. If a mismatch is reported, the statement can move to Disputed or Adjusted with a visible audit trail.
  • Outputs: Downloadable GST/Tax compliant commission statement, invoice reference, and payout status.
  • Failure Points: Payout mismatches due to mid-cycle refunds; blocked bank transfers; disputed attribution affecting final payable.

5.10 Support and Issue Escalation

  • Goal: Resolve partner queries quickly via a dedicated helpdesk.
  • Trigger: Partner encounters a dashboard issue, payout dispute, or institution mapping error.
  • Main Actions: Partner clicks "Raise Helpdesk Ticket".
  • System Behavior: Routes ticket to Tier-2 Partner Operations queue.
  • Outputs: Ticket ID, SLA tracking notification.
  • Failure Points: Helpdesk flooded with student-level tech issues incorrectly raised by the partner.

6. Dashboard UX Requirements

The partner dashboard must be clean, data-driven, and focused on institutional management and financial tracking.

  • Navigation Modules: Home (Overview), Campaigns & Links, Assigned Institutions, Student Roster, Analytics & Results, Finance & Payouts, Helpdesk.
  • Key Screens:
    • Dashboard Home: High-level metrics (Assigned Institutions, Total clicks, Total Signups, Paid Conversions, Revenue Generated, Pending Payout).
    • Institution Detail View: Drill down into a specific school's performance, registration pipeline, slot status, and result summary.
    • Campaign Detail View: Link-level attribution, expiry date, conversion rate, and last activity.
    • Finance & Statements: Statement lifecycle, deductions, dispute actions, and downloadable PDFs/CSVs.
  • Widgets/Cards: Quick-copy link card, upcoming exam dates countdown, recent payout status, active disputes, institution health score, and top-performing campaign.
  • Filters/Search: Global search by Institution Name or Student ID. Date range filters for financial reporting.
  • Alerts/Notifications: Top-right bell icon for mapped institution changes, result publishing, and payment settlements.
  • Exports/Downloads: Universal "Export to CSV" for tables, PDF download for commission statements and aggregate school performance reports.

10. Data and Reporting Requirements

Reports must be generated with timestamp accuracy and downloadable in CSV format.

  • Institution Registration Report: List of assigned schools, total invited, total registered, conversion %.
  • Student Attribution Log: Student ID, associated Partner Link, Date of Registration, Payment Status (Success/Refunded).
  • Commission Lifecycle Statement: Gross attributed revenue, applicable commission rate, deducted refunds, net payable, payout status.
  • Exam Performance Export: Institution Name, Grade, Student ID, Scorecard link, Rank/Percentile.

11. Notifications and Communication Events

Triggered via Email and Dashboard In-App Alerts:

  • Account Approved: Sent upon Admin approval of partner application.
  • New Institution Assigned: Sent when Admin adds a school to the partner's portfolio.
  • First Conversion Alert: Sent when the first student successfully registers via a new link.
  • Results Published: Notification that scorecards and analytics are ready for download.
  • Commission Statement Generated: Monthly or post-cycle alert that financials are ready for review.

12. Exceptions, Risks, and Blind Spots

Operational risks specific to the partner workflow that require mitigation:

  • Duplicate Mapping / Disputed Attribution: If a student clicks Partner A's link but later registers via Partner B's link, the system must follow a strict "last-click" or "first-click" attribution rule (TBD by business).
  • Refunds Affecting Commissions: If a student requests a refund after the commission statement is generated, the system must process a "clawback" or negative balance on the subsequent statement.
  • Expired Links: Partners utilizing links for exams where the registration window has closed. The link must redirect to a graceful "Registration Closed" landing page.
  • Data Privacy: Partners viewing detailed analytics of students. Ensure Terms & Conditions explicitly grant this right during student registration.

Audit Trail Recommendation:

It is strictly recommended that the system maintains an immutable audit log for all partner attribution events (link creation, student click, payment timestamp, refund trigger) and payout state changes (Pending -> Calculated -> Settled). This is critical for resolving financial disputes with partners.

14. Partner FAQs by Stage

Stage: Onboarding & Account Setup

  • How do I reset my password if I lose access? (Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page; an email will be sent).
  • How do I update my organization's banking details? (Navigate to Profile Settings > Financials. Changes require Admin approval).

Stage: Campaign Links & Institution Management

  • An institution is missing from my dashboard. How do I add them? (Partners cannot add institutions directly. Raise a ticket with the helpdesk to request mapping).
  • Does my referral link expire? (Links remain active until the global registration deadline for the specific Olympiad exam).

Stage: Conversion Tracking

  • A student registered but is not showing on my dashboard? (Conversions only reflect after a successful direct online payment. Pending payments do not show).
  • How often is the conversion dashboard updated? (Registration and conversion data is updated in real-time).

Stage: Payouts & Commissions

  • Why is my commission lower than my total conversions? (If students requested refunds before the cutoff, those are deducted from your gross conversions).
  • When can I download my commission statement? (Statements are generated automatically 5 business days after the exam window closes).

Stage: Support

  • Who do I contact if there is a discrepancy in my payout? (Use the "Raise Helpdesk Ticket" module in your dashboard, categorized under 'Payout Dispute').

15. Open Decisions / Product Questions

  • Attribution Model: Will the platform utilize First-Click or Last-Click attribution if a student interacts with multiple partner links?
  • Clawback Policy: How exactly will the system handle mid-cycle refunds from students? Does it deduct immediately from the partner's active balance or wait for the next billing cycle?
  • Institution Ownership: Can multiple partners be assigned to the same institution for different exams, or is it an exclusive relationship?
